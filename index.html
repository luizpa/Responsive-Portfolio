About Me

Born and raised in Peru. Decided to move to the US in ’04 and landed in NJ when I was 19 years old. With a new culture, language and not knowing many people I made my way through it. I currently live in Charlotte NC and work as a painting contractor with Four Seasons Painting, a company I own. I feel capable of doing many things, anything I set my mind to. That’s why I decided to switch careers and start a journey as a web developer.

I chose web developing because that’s where we’re heading. As technology develops at this pace soon, this language will become a basic need. With that said, it’s also very big field that allows me to be creative in many ways. Web developing is a part of my life now and as every day goes by I’m learning something new.

Through my portfolio you’ll find different projects I’ve done and you can also get it touch with me.
